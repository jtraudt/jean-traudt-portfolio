Milwaukee River Watersheds

For my final project in Intro to Cartography, I was inspired to create a map highlighting the 6 watersheds of the Milwaukee River Basin. In high school, I took a class called "Watershed Wisdom" that combined english, environmental science, and art. After the school year, we biked 80 miles north of Milwaukee along the Milwaukee River and canoed back home. I used ArcGIS Pro and Adobe Illustrator to create this map.
